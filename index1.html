How to Play Tic-Tac-Toe

  1. Setup:
    • Draw a 3x3 grid.
    • Decide which player will be 'X' and which will be 'O'.
  2. Gameplay:
    • Players take turns placing their symbol ('X' or 'O') on an empty spot.
    • 'X' typically goes first.
  3. Objective:
    • Get three of your symbols in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) to win.
  4. Taking Turns:
    • Alternate turns, placing your symbol on an empty space each turn.
  5. Winning:
    • Win by placing three of your symbols in a row before your opponent.
    • The game ends immediately once a player wins.
  6. Ending the Game:
    • The game ends if a player wins or if all cells are filled without a winner.
  7. Example Gameplay:
    • Player 1 ('X') places a symbol.
    • Player 2 ('O') places a symbol.
    • Continue until someone wins or the game ends in a draw.
  8. Winning Combinations:
    • Three symbols in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  9. Strategy:
    • Pay attention to your opponent's moves to block them and create your winning sequence.
